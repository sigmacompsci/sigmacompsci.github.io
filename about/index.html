About Us


Welcome to the website of SigmaCS!

The Sigma Computer Science team is a group of friends who enjoy programming, math, and science. We compete in local hackathons and lead our school's computer science club, BRCS. We also are active developers and coders who upload our code to GitHub. We are proficient in Python, JavaScript, C, HTML, CSS, and SQL.
