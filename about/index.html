About Us

Welcome to the website of SigmaCS!

Sigma Computer Science is a group of friends who enjoy programming and computer science. We believe that applications should be visually appealing, simple to use, and function well and quickly. Therefore, our projects and programs all exemplify this belief by having minimalistic yet aesthetically pleasing graphics and user interfaces, while also successfully and quickly completing the task at hand. We also lead our school's computer science club, BRCS. Languages we use include Python, JavaScript, C, HTML, and CSS.
