Sigma Computer Science

2x hackathon winners, BRCS club founders and leaders, and developers of 'Graze'

Sigma Computer Science is a hackathon team consisting of Alex Sheng (intrepidbird), Dhruv Rakhade (pointySphere, and Tharun Naguleswaran (Cellius-1). We participate in local hackathons across New Jersey, and enjoy programming and coding in general. Alex and Dhruv are founders of the Bridgewater-Raritan Computer Science Club, and Tharun is the Webmaster of BRCS. We primarily consist of these three members, but occasionally open up a fourth spot in select hackathons.
