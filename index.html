Sigma Computer Science

A hackathon team in New Jersey

Sigma Computer Science is a hackathon team consisting of Alex Sheng (intrepidbird), Dhruv Rakhade (pointySphere), Tharun Naguleswaran (Cellius-1), Jay Fang (FoxJavaGauss), and Anshuman Roy (isAnshTHEmaN). We participate in hackathons across New Jersey, and enjoy programming and computer science in general. Alex, Dhruv, and Jay are founders of the Bridgewater-Raritan Computer Science Club, and Tharun is the Webmaster of BRCS. We have developed praised and award-winning projects, such as the SigmaCS Trilogy.
